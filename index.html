About Me

A deputy store manager at Aldi ltd who has spent five years working in retail, is now looking for a career change into IT. With an IT degree obtained at University of East London back in 2014, I want to Go from merchandising and stacking shelves to learning a full stack of front-end languages. To do this I aim to create clean, responsive, user-friendly, web sites and incorporate core front end fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
I enjoy problem solving through rigorous testing and gaining a sense of achievement when problems are solved regardless of its severity. Doing this allows for easy updating of code, debugging errors and keeping code readable for other developers.

At present I'm undertaking a front-end development course provided by the learning people where I will be taking my first steps in trying to achieve my goal. This is through a combination of real-life projects and intensive course content.
